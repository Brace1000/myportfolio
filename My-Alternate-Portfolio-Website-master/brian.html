My Story

I am a driven and enthusiastic Junior Software Developer currently training at Zone01 Kisumu Digital Training Centre, Through zone01 open projects in app and web development, I’ve had the opportunity to make a tangible difference in Amherst, bringing accessible technology and innovative solutions to community-driven initiatives. Beyond development, I contribute as a Designer on the Nairobit Organizing Team, where I’m responsible for the visual identity of one of the largest hackathons in the region. This role has sharpened my graphic design abilities and deepened my appreciation for the collaborative, fast-paced environment of tech events, Coordinating with cross-functional teams, I ensure our designs capture the excitement and inclusivity of the hackathon experience. My journey is fueled by a passion for learning and a dedication to community impact. I am constantly expanding my skill set, from backend programming to UI/UX design, and am excited to continue growing as a software developer. I look forward to contributing my skills, creativity, and collaborative spirit to projects that make a positive impact.
